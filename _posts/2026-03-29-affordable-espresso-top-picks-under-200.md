### What to Look for in an Espresso Machine
Before we dive into our top picks, it's essential to consider the key factors that make an espresso machine great. These include:
* Pressure: A good espresso machine should have a minimum of 9 atmospheres of pressure to produce a proper espresso shot.
* Temperature control: The ideal temperature for brewing espresso is between 195°F and 205°F.
* Ease of use: A user-friendly interface and simple operation can make a big difference in your daily coffee routine.
* Durability: A well-built machine with high-quality materials can withstand regular use and last for a long time.
* Additional features: Some machines may come with extra features like built-in grinders, frothing capabilities, or automatic shut-off.

### Maintenance and Cleaning
To ensure your espresso machine lasts for a long time, it's essential to regular maintenance and cleaning. This includes:
* **Descale regularly**: Mineral buildup can damage your machine, so it's essential to descale it every 3-6 months.
* **Clean the brew group**: The brew group is the part of the machine where the coffee grounds come into contact with the water. Cleaning it regularly can help prevent old coffee oils and residue from affecting the taste of your coffee.
* **Run a cleaning cycle**: Many espresso machines come with a cleaning cycle that can help remove old coffee oils and residue from the machine.
